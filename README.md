# Task 02: Brand Persona & Voice Guide – EcoSip 

## Overview
This repository contains my submission for Task 02 of my Digital Marketing Internship. The objective is to create a Brand Persona and Brand Voice Guide for EcoSip.

## About EcoSip
EcoSip is an eco-friendly beverage brand focused on sustainability, health, and reducing plastic waste. It promotes a green lifestyle and encourages the use of reusable products.

## Brand Persona
EcoSip is a modern, responsible, and eco-conscious brand. It targets students, professionals, fitness lovers, and people who care about the environment and sustainable living.

## Brand Voice
The brand voice is:
- Friendly
- Positive
- Simple
- Motivational

It focuses on clear communication and inspiring people to adopt eco-friendly habits in daily life.

## Key Values
- Sustainability
- Health & Wellness
- Eco-Friendly Innovation
- Quality & Trust
- Community Awareness

## Learning Outcome
This task helped me understand how to build a strong brand identity and maintain consistent communication. It improved my skills in branding, audience targeting, and digital marketing strategy.
